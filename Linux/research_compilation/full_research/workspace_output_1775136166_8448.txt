     KDMapper
     https://github.com/TheCruZ/kdmapper

     Exploits iqvw64e.sys Intel driver CVE-2015-229 to manually map non-signed drivers in
     kernel memory.

     Automatically clears major Traces such as MmUnloadedDrivers, PiDDBCacheTable &
     g_KernelHashBucketList but not all like PoolBigPageTable.

     Loading driver is easy: kdmapper.exe driver.sys
